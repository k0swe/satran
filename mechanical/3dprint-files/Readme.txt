Gears recommended print settings
---
ASA, ABS or Nylon
0.4mm nozzle
0.15mm layer height
60-70% infill
30-40mm/s print speed
Gears should have a tight fit on to the motor or sensor axle. 
To increase/decrease play, adjust the
infill by 5-10% or resize the entire part by a few percent.

Housing recommended print settings
---
PLA or PETG
0.4mm nozzle
0.2mm layer height
50-60% infill
40-60mm/s print speed
Good luck!
